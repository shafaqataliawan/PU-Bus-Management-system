University of the Punjab

The University of the Punjab provides affordable transport for students and staff across Lahore. It runs 55 buses, including a shuttle between Allama Iqbal and Quaid-e-Azam campuses. Transport is also available for trips like picnics and study tours. The university works to make travel easy and comfortable for everyone.

University of the Punjab

Established in Lahore in 1882, the University of Punjab is the largest and the oldest seat of higher learning in Pakistan. It was the first to be established in the sub-continent in a Muslim majority area.
